Taiwan SDGs Award

What is the Taiwan SDGs Award?

To encourage people from all walks of life to actively take part in sustainable development efforts, the NCSD has organized the National Sustainable Development Award every year since 2004 to recognize schools, businesses, NGOs, and government agencies that have made outstanding contributions to the goal of sustainable development and helped raise awareness nationwide. The list of award winners over the years can be found on the NCSD website.
